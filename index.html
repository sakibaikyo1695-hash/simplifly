📖 USER GUIDE — SIMPLIFLY

⚠️ SIMPLIFLY is a planning aid only. It is NOT a certified navigation system. Always use approved charts and instruments as primary references.
1. GETTING STARTED

Open the app by tapping the SIMPLIFLY APK installed on your device. Sign in with your registered account — new accounts require admin approval before access is granted. The sidebar is on the right — tap the blue tab on the edge to collapse or expand it for a full-screen map view.

2. MAP LAYERS

Tap the 🛰️ SATELLITE button on the top-left of the map to instantly toggle between Normal and Satellite view. Additional layers are available: ⛰️ Topo (terrain contours), 🌑 Dark (low-light), and 🗾 ESRI Topo. Normal (OpenStreetMap) is recommended for most VFR planning.

3. ADDING WAYPOINTS (FIXES)

By tapping the map: Tap any location → confirm → enter the fix name (e.g. AGAXY, VOR name, airfield ICAO) and any notes.
By coordinates: Enter Lat/Lon in the Jump to Coordinates section using degrees and decimal minutes (DMS format) → tap 🎯 GO / ADD WAYPOINT.
Marker types: ■ Airport (green), H Helipad (blue), ★ Compulsory reporting point (gold), ◆ Checkpoint (silver), ● Fix/Waypoint (red), ▲ Position (white).

4. WAYPOINT LIST & SEARCH

Tap 📋 LIST to open the full waypoints list. Use the search bar to filter by name instantly. From the list you can Edit, Delete, Select for routing, or open stored Approach Plates for that fix.

5. APPROACH PLATES (PDFs)

Each waypoint/airport can store one or more approach plate PDFs. In the Edit Waypoint modal, scroll to 📄 APPROACH PLATES → tap ➕ ADD PLATE → select your PDF files. They are stored locally on the device.
Viewing: Tap the marker on the map → tap 📄 Plates, or use the waypoint list. The full-screen viewer shows all pages with PREV / NEXT navigation.

6. ROUTE PLANNING

Select 2 or more waypoints (tap markers or use 🎯 Sel in the list — they highlight blue). Enter your cruise speed in knots in the SPEED field. Tap ⛓ ROUTE to draw the route. Each leg shows: distance (NM), magnetic bearing, and estimated time. Tap 💾 SAVE ROUTE to store it for reuse. Load saved routes via 📂 SAVED ROUTES.

7. GPS TRACKING (AIRBORNE)

Tap ✈️ FLY ON to activate live GPS. Your aircraft position is shown on the map and updates continuously. GPS Ground Speed and Track are displayed. Tap 📡 TRACK ON to project a 20-minute track line ahead based on current speed and course.
Note: GPS requires location permission to be granted on your device. Allow location access when prompted after installing the APK.

8. DIRECT-TO NAVIGATION

In Flying Mode, select a destination from the Direct-To dropdown → tap ➤ DIRECT-TO. A dashed line draws from your current GPS position to the waypoint, showing live distance, bearing, and ETA. Tap ⏹ STOP to cancel. This is useful for quick diversions.

9. AIRSPACE & AREA MANAGEMENT

Tap ✚ CREATE AREA to draw a polygon or circular area (useful for marking CTRs, restricted airspace, danger areas, or operating zones). Enter corner coordinates or choose a centre waypoint and radius. Use the Show / Hide toggles to manage clutter on the map.

10. MEASUREMENT TOOL

Tap 📏 MEASURE on the map. Two draggable markers appear. Drag them to measure distance and reciprocal bearings between any two points — useful for checking distances to airspace boundaries or alternates. Tap ⏹ STOP when done.

11. OFFLINE MAP CACHING (PRE-FLIGHT)

Before flying into a low-connectivity area, expand Pre-flight Cache → tap 📦 CACHE AREA → draw a rectangle over your planned area on the map → select zoom levels (Z12–Z15 recommended for VFR) and map layers → tap ⬇ DOWNLOAD NOW. Cached tiles remain available when offline.

12. FUEL CALCULATOR

Expand the ⛽ Fuel Calculator section. Enter your aircraft's fuel flow (kg/hr), maximum fuel capacity, current fuel on board, and planned uplift. The calculator shows total after uplift, 5% safety reserve, usable fuel, and estimated endurance.

13. FLIGHT BRIEF & ROUTE SHEET

Tap 🖨️ PRINT BRIEF. Fill in flight details (callsign/flight no., PIC/SIC, aircraft type, ETD, altitude, frequencies), weather, NOTAMs, and special instructions on Step 1. Step 2 calculates route distances, bearings, and fuel. Step 3 generates a printable briefing sheet — tap 🖨️ PRINT FLIGHT BRIEF and use your device's print/save-as-PDF dialog.

14. IMPORT / EXPORT & SHARING

💾 SAVE: Saves all data to this device's local storage.
📤 EXPORT: Export waypoints, areas, routes, or a custom mix as a JSON file. Include approach plate PDFs if needed (increases file size). Use 🛣️ Share Routes to export a route with all its waypoints.
📥 IMPORT: Paste a previously exported JSON and tap IMPORT to restore.
💬 Pilot Chat: Use the chat feature to share waypoints, areas, and routes directly to other SIMPLIFLY users in real time.

15. DOCUMENTS LIBRARY

Store PDFs such as AFM extracts, SOPs, Jeppesen charts, or company documents under 📄 Documents. Tap ➕ ADD DOCUMENT, select your PDF, give it a name, and save. Documents are stored on-device and accessible offline. Tap the document name to open the full-screen viewer.

16. TIPS FOR TABLET & MOBILE USE

• Install the SIMPLIFLY APK on your Android device or tablet for the best full-screen experience.
• Grant Location Permission when prompted — required for GPS tracking and Direct-To navigation.
• Collapse the sidebar with the edge tab for a full-screen map view when airborne.
• Pre-cache map tiles on the ground before departure — cached tiles work fully offline in the APK.
• After printing a brief, use the in-app ◀ BACK button — do not close the app.
